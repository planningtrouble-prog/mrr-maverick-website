Revenue Engineer™
Revenue Engineering is the discipline of deliberately architecting your revenue system instead of letting it organically evolve:

—Which channels you use (PLG, SLG, partners, platforms, affiliates)
—What role each channel plays
—Who owns the customer relationship
—How pricing works across channels
—Where you allow distribution - and where you don't
—How you protect your highest-value revenue streams
Post-PMF
